# AI Shockwave Index

An economic analysis of how major AI events impacted financial markets across industries.

## What This Project Does
Measures the market impact of 6 major AI milestones across 12 companies, 
organized into three categories: AI Winners, Disrupted Companies, and Infrastructure.

## Key Events Analyzed
- ChatGPT Launch (Nov 2022)
- GPT-4 Release (Mar 2023)
- Nvidia $1T Market Cap (May 2023)
- Sam Altman Firing & Rehiring (Nov 2023)
- DeepSeek R1 Launch (Jan 2025)
- Meta $65B AI Investment (Jan 2025)

## Key Findings
- Infrastructure companies were the most consistent winners across all events
- Disrupted companies (Chegg, Upwork, Fiverr) showed sustained negative impact
- DeepSeek caused the most dramatic single-event market movement
- Palantir emerged as the strongest infrastructure beneficiary across multiple events
